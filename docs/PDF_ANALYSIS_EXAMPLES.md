## Troubleshooting

### "I'm not getting detailed enough responses"
**Solution**: Be more specific in your prompt
- Instead of: "Summarize this"
- Try: "Provide a detailed summary including methodology, findings, and conclusions"

### "The AI missed important information"
**Solution**: Ask follow-up questions
- "What about [specific topic]?"
- "Can you elaborate on [section]?"
- "What does page X say about [topic]?"

### "Response is too long"
**Solution**: Request concise format
- "Give me a brief summary"
- "List only the top 5 key points"
- "Provide a one-paragraph overview"

### "I need information from a specific section"
**Solution**: Reference the section
- "Summarize the conclusion section"
- "What does the introduction say about [topic]?"
- "Focus on pages 10-15"

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I upload multiple PDFs at once?**
A: Currently, you can upload one PDF per message. For multiple documents, upload them in separate messages.

**Q: How long does processing take?**
A: Usually 3-10 seconds, depending on document length.

**Q: Can it read scanned PDFs?**
A: Text extraction works best with text-based PDFs. Scanned documents (images) may have limited support.

**Q: Is my document stored?**
A: Documents are processed temporarily and not permanently stored on servers.

**Q: What's the maximum file size?**
A: 10MB per PDF file.

**Q: Can it analyze tables and charts?**
A: Currently focuses on text content. Visual elements may not be fully analyzed.
